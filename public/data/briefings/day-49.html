Coercive Diplomacy Under a Blockade: CENTCOM Tallies, BBC Verify Dissents, “Locked and Loaded”

The blockade kept turning hulls around and still failed to match its rhetoric. Brad Cooper can credibly cite when describing the blockade as “fully implemented.” CENTCOM reports more than 10,000 sailors, Marines, and airmen enforcing the line with “more than a dozen warships and dozens of aircraft.” But BBC Verify, working with MarineTraffic and gCaptain, explicitly disputes the “zero penetrations” claim: at least 4 Iran-related ships transited the Strait Apr 14. Lebanon: Nabatieh Strike, Ashdod Naval Base, and an Israeli Cabinet That Could Not Decide

IDF airstrikes hit a vehicle in Jiyeh south of Beirut and the village of Qlaileh near Tyre on April 15.
