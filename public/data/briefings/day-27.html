His elimination, based on joint US-Israeli intelligence, decapitates the IRGC naval command structure at the exact moment Iran was attempting to leverage Hormuz control as a negotiating asset.

The timing is significant. Iran’s 5-point counterproposal explicitly demands “recognition of Iran’s sovereignty over the Strait of Hormuz,” and its parliament is drafting legislation to formally codify this claim.
