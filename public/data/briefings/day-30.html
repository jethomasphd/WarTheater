The Twin Chokepoint Crisis: Hormuz and Bab al-Mandeb

The Houthi entry into the war continues to reshape the strategic calculus. The Strait of Hormuz itself remains effectively closed, with traffic down 95%+ from pre-war levels. Only 142 total transits occurred March 1–25 versus a normal approximately 3,800+.
